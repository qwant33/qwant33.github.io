Gallery Sider

It Functions As Such:

  • There is a main view where thumbnail images you select will be displayed full size
  • The bottom bar hosts images from a gallery to be selected and displayed
  • Each thumbnail slides with the click and drag motion or arrows on each side.
  • The thumbnails are inset on a card.
  • When hovering over thumbnails there will be a choice to sign in or make an account
  • Being signed in will make this overlay disapear
